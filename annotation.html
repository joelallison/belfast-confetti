ANNOTATION

Key Definitions:

  • Fount: a spring or fountain; or, variant spelling of font [line 3]
  • Type: characters or letters that are printed or shown on a screen
  • Balaclava: Battle in 1854 during the Crimean War; or, headgear that covers most of the face
  • Raglan: Commander in Battle of Balaclava
  • Inkerman: Battle in 1854 during the Crimean War
  • Odessa: Siege during WW2
  • Crimea: Autonomous republic of Ukraine
  • Saracen(s): (Medieval) a people distinguishable from those known as Arabs. By 12th Century synonymous with ‘Muslim’; or, 6-wheeled armoured truck used during the Troubles
  • Kremlin-2 mesh: protective metal grate for armoured trucks
  • Makrolon face-shields: face guard for riot police
  • Fusillade: a round of bullets fired by several guns at once
